About Us

The Godly Men Ministries International formerly known as The Godly Men Group is a novel organization inspired by the Holy Spirit birthed to lure men away from the modern generational decadence into a life of service, royalty and priesthood. Men who will standout in every area of their lives and be role models to generations.

Our sister ministry: Dawn Academy for Men is a CHRIST-centered institution for intensive training for Men.

God is yelling out to the men of this generation. He wants their hearts turned back to Him. A revival has begun for the souls of men and, this ministry is a vessel the Lord is using.


OUR VISION:

  • To raise generations of Godly men.
  • To reach out to every man on the surface of the earth, and moulding them to be AS Christ.
  • To build on what God has called them to be- Priests, Purposeful fathers, husbands and sons.
  • To encourage younger men to uphold Godly values and pass it on to their generations to come.
  • To reach out to young boys, teenagers, youths, single men, fathers and every man in every strata of society, teaching them about God, purpose and life.



OUR MISSION:

To spread the gospel of Jesus and Godly values through full gospel teachings of the Word of God, experiencing the joy of the faith and living in the reality of Christ.
